I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the 
[Bellini College of Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Computing (CAICC)](https://www.usf.edu/ai-cybersecurity-computing/) 
at the [University of South Florida](https://www.usf.edu/).

My work studies the security risks and misuse of modern AI systems and large-scale cloud infrastructures. In particular, I investigate vulnerabilities in LLM-based systems, Wi-Fi geolocation services, and serverless computing environments.

## Research Interests

- Security and misuse of large language models
- Wi-Fi positioning system security
- Co-location risks in Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) platforms
