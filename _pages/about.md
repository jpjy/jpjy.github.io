I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the **University of South Florida (USF)**. My research focuses on **AI security, large language model (LLM) safety, and cloud systems security**.

My work studies the security risks and misuse of modern AI systems and large-scale cloud infrastructures. In particular, I investigate vulnerabilities in LLM-based systems, Wi-Fi geolocation services, and serverless computing environments.

## Research Interests

- Security and misuse of large language models
- AI system security and LLM safety
- Wi-Fi positioning system security
- Side-channel attacks in Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) environments
- Cloud and distributed systems security
